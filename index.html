About

Two homes, one house

WOORIZIP means "our home" in Korean, and that's the whole plan for the place: a comfortable, practical base for anyone passing through Penukonda — long-stay professionals from the Kia Motors corridor as much as families on the road.

Rooms are simple and kept properly clean. The dining hall runs a table that understands both a Korean craving and a Telugu one. Downstairs, a full home gym and a table-tennis table mean a long day doesn't have to end the moment you get back.

We're easy to find — just behind the Nayara petrol pump in Gutturu, close to Penukonda's main routes.
